    Insurance

    Do you have an existing insurance policy you have not reviewed for a while? At A&T Financial Advisers we can review your existing policies to ensure they remain appropriate to your current situation. Most of us have insurance for our car and our home, and health insurance to cover time in hospital and visits to the dentist or optometrist. Many, however, don't insure themselves against the risk of losing their life, or their income, either temporarily or even worse, permanently. Could you absorb the costs associated with a serious health incident?

    Our financial planners can help you get on the right path in achieving your financial goals. We provide advice on:

    • Review Existing Policies
    • Life Insurance
    • TPD (Total Permanent Disability) Insurance
    • Trauma/Critical Illness insurance
    • Income Protection
