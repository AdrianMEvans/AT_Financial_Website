About Us

Our advisers can help you get on the right path in achieving your financial goals. We provide advice on:

  • Superannuation (including Self Managed Superannuation Funds)
  • Insurance - including Life, TPD, Trauma & Income protection
  • Insurance Claims
  • Retirement Planning
  • Investments and Wealth Creation

At A&T Financial Advisers our mission is to provide strategies to enhance your financial position and assist on your journey to wealth creation. We are a non-aligned advisory firm and not restricted by any affiliations to financial institutions or product providers. We pride ourselves on developing long-term cohesive relationships with our clients helping them achieve their financial goals and security.


We believe that there is no "one size fits all" solution to financial planning as each person's situation is unique, therefore our approach comprises customisation and tailoring of our financial advice solutions to match the individual client's objectives.

Services

As qualified financial advisers, we are specialists in Insurance, Investment and Superannuation advice. We don’t accept a “one size fits all” service offering. We tailor our approach to you and your goals. Let us create a plan based on your preferences and what stage you are in your financial lifstyle.

Tim Vander Kraats

Tim is a Financial Adviser and Chartered Accountant, having completed his Bachelor of Commerce Degree majoring in Accounting and Finance from Notre Dame and admitted to the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Australia and New Zealand in 2011. Tim started his career in accounting at Athans & Taylor Joondalup in 2007 and currently practices as a Financial Adviser (Director) of A&T Financial Advisers, specialising in superannuation, retirement planning and risk advice. Tim is accredited as a CA SMSF Specialist and serves as a committee member for the specialist group.

Tim is an Authorised Representative of Synchron (AFSL 243313) and Registered Tax (Financial) Adviser with the Tax Practitioners Board.

Geoffrey Luck

Geoff is a Chartered Accountant having been admitted to the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Australia and New Zealand in 1982 and is also a licensed Financial Adviser. Geoff commenced his business career with Peat Marwick Mitchell and Co. (now known as KPMG) in their audit division and spent nine years within that discipline including two years in overseas offices. Geoff spent a further two years as manager within their business services division before leaving to join Athans & Taylor Joondalup. Geoff is currently the managing director of A&T Financial Advisers focussing on wealth creation and retirement planning.

Geoff is an Authorised Representative of Synchron (AFSL 243313) and Registered Tax Agent with the Tax Practitioners Board.
