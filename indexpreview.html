About Us

A & T Financial Advisers was established in early 2014 as an offshoot of the accounting practice of A & T Accountants and Advisers. At that time with legislative changes and other factors it was becoming increasingly obvious that clients wanted trusted but specialist financial advice particularly concerning retirement planning. We believed we had a solid foundation to service this market and our approach would be without the conflicted positions which were all too common in the industry in those days. Since then the firm has expanded and consolidated with other financial planning practices to form a practice which offers a full range of financial services. Whilst the firm has grown and changed we still believe that there is no "one size fits all" solution.

Each person's situation is unique, and therefore our approach comprises customisation and tailoring of our financial advice solutions to match the individual client's objectives. This is reflected in our motto “Planning for Your Success”. At A&T Financial Advisers our mission is to provide strategies to enhance your financial position and assist on your journey to wealth creation. We are a non-aligned advisory firm and not restricted by any affiliations to financial institutions or product providers. We pride ourselves on developing long-term cohesive relationships with our clients helping them achieve their financial goals and security.
